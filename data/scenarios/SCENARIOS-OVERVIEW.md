## How to Use

**Remember: You are the seller, the AI is your customer.**

The application will automatically load all scenarios. When you start the app:

1. You'll see 9 Swiss health insurance scenarios (3 in German, 3 in French, 3 in English)
2. Select the scenario that matches your training needs and language preference
3. Click "Start Training" to begin the role-play session
4. Practice your sales skills by responding to the AI customer's questions and concerns

## Scenario Types

Each language has three scenario types:
1. **Premium Consultation** - Customer seeking advice on premium increases and deductible options
2. **Supplementary Insurance** - Customer inquiring about additional coverage for family
3. **Objection Handling** - Skeptical customer with concerns about switching providers

## Evaluation Criteria

All scenarios are evaluated on:
- **Speaking Tone & Style (30 points)**: Professional tone, active listening, engagement quality
- **Conversation Content Quality (70 points)**: Needs assessment, value proposition, objection handling

## Swiss Market Specifics

- Franchise options: CHF 300, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 2500
- Basic insurance (Grundversicherung/Assurance de base): Mandatory, identical across providers
- Supplementary insurance: Separate contracts, optional
- No pre-existing condition exclusions for basic insurance
- Realistic Swiss pricing and locations
